Final Year Project

CLUSTER BOMB DETECTOR.

Which real world Problem shall be solved by this project?

  • Difficulty in finding the cluster bombs in fields and hilly areas.

    • Purpose:

    Cluster bombs have cost the lives of hundreds in our country. In this project we will create an aerial vehicle used as a complemented tool for cluster bomb detection in difficult scenarios. The prototype developed by us helps to detect a cluster bombs using a flying drone. The prototype has a quadcopter which has a cluster bomb detector mounted on it. This utilizes two different modes of detection, which are an IR(thermal) camera and a metal detector. These are extensively used in aiding this whole operation. The location of the mine can be traced back by the GPS and the detected location can be transmitted using the GSM module. There is a lot of untapped potential and scope of improvement for this prototype in the future. The goal is not only to detect fully visible cluster bombs but also those partially buried or hidden. It has been observed an effective percentage of the detection over 80% when flying at low altitudes about 1m from the ground at speeds up to 8km/h. We will make a design in which drone can pass through small paths and can detect obstacles in real life minimize crashing.
